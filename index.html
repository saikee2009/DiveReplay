DiveReplay — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-Jun-17

DiveReplay is a video review tool. All video and audio it records are processed and stored on your device, and shared only directly between your own nearby devices over the local network. They are never sent to the developer or to any server.

Data collected by the developer: none. The app has no account, no analytics, and no advertising.

You are responsible for obtaining any consent required before recording other people — including written parental or guardian consent for anyone under 18 — and for following the rules of any venue or club where you record.

The app is not directed at children. Recordings may include minors only where you have the necessary consent.
